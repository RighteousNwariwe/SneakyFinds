About Us

Welcome to SneakyFinds, your ultimate online thrift store bringing you "Good Prices, Great Steals!"

We’re based in the heart of Vaal and Johannesburg East, proudly curating a wide selection of trendy, affordable, and high-quality pre-loved items. At SneakyFinds, we believe that thrift shopping should be fun, accessible, and a great way to embrace sustainability without breaking the bank.

Why Choose Us?

  • Affordable Prices: Amazing deals that fit your budget.
  • Wide Range: From vintage gems to modern essentials, we’ve got it all!
  • Convenience: Shop from the comfort of your home and have your finds delivered to your door.

Delivery Information

We deliver across Gauteng at an extra fee, but here’s the kicker—delivery is free for students around NWU Vaal Campus and VUT! We’re all about supporting our local student community, so you can focus on hitting the books while we handle your thrifting needs.

Join the SneakyFinds community today and discover amazing steals that are just a click away. Let’s make thrift shopping stylish, affordable, and unforgettable!
